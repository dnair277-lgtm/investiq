What is this? The S&P 500 tracks America's 500 biggest companies. It's used as a benchmark — if you beat it, you're doing better than most professional fund managers!
About the S&P 500

The S&P 500 Index (ticker: ^GSPC) is the gold standard benchmark for U.S. stock market performance. Studies show that over 90% of active fund managers fail to beat it over any 15-year period. If your portfolio return is higher than the S&P 500 return, you're beating the pros!

How the Leaderboard Works
📊 Ranking Metric
Players are ranked by Portfolio Return % — your total P&L divided by your starting capital. This levels the playing field regardless of how much you invest.
🔒 Privacy First
Participation is opt-in only. Enable sharing in Settings → Leaderboard. You control your display name — your real name is never shared.
🏅 Badges
Earn badges based on your return: Rookie (0–5%), Apprentice (5–15%), Pro (15–30%), Expert (30–50%), Legend (50%+). Beat the S&P 500 to earn the ⚡ Market Beater badge.

About InvestIQ

InvestIQ is a personal investment tracker built for teens learning to invest responsibly. All your data is stored locally on your device only — nothing is ever sent to a server.

Tip: Get a free Finnhub API key at finnhub.io/register (takes 1 min) — it unlocks live price updates, daily % change, and real news headlines for all your tickers.
